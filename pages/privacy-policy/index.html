DC Metro Mate Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

DC Metro Mate is an iPhone app for viewing Washington Metro rail arrival information, station departures, service alerts, and a home screen widget. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Summary

  • DC Metro Mate does not require an account.
  • DC Metro Mate does not sell personal information.
  • DC Metro Mate does not show ads.
  • DC Metro Mate does not track you across apps or websites.
  • Your selected station and preferred line are stored on your device.

Information Stored On Your Device

DC Metro Mate stores a small amount of app preference data locally on your device so the app and widget can remember what to show. This includes:

  • Your most commonly selected Metro stations.
  • Your preferred line for a selected station.
  • A recent widget snapshot, such as station name and arrival predictions.

This information is stored in the app's private device storage and, for the widget, in the app's iOS App Group storage. It is not used to identify you and is not sold. It is removed if you delete the app from your device.

Metro Data Requests

To show train arrivals and service alerts, DC Metro Mate contacts the DC Metro Mate API proxy hosted on Cloudflare. That proxy requests rail data from the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority (WMATA) API.

These requests may include the station code needed to retrieve arrival predictions. Like most internet services, Cloudflare may process standard technical information such as IP address, request time, user agent, and request URL for delivery, security, reliability, and abuse prevention. DC Metro Mate does not use this information to build a personal profile, sell data, or track you across apps or websites.

Information We Do Not Collect

DC Metro Mate does not ask for or collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.
  • Precise GPS location.
  • Contacts, photos, calendars, or messages.
  • Payment information.
  • Advertising identifiers.

Analytics, Advertising, And Tracking

DC Metro Mate does not currently include third-party advertising SDKs, third-party analytics SDKs, or cross-app tracking. If this changes in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy details will be updated.

Data Sharing

DC Metro Mate does not sell your information. DC Metro Mate uses Cloudflare to host the API proxy and uses WMATA data sources to provide transit information. These services are used to operate the app's core functionality.

Data Retention

Local preferences remain on your device until you change them, clear app data, or delete the app. Technical request data processed by Cloudflare is handled according to Cloudflare's service configuration and policies.

Children's Privacy

DC Metro Mate is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated as DC Metro Mate changes. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.
