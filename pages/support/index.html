MetroLens Support

MetroLens helps riders view Washington Metro rail arrivals, service alerts, and station information from the iPhone app and widgets.

Contact

For support, bug reports, or privacy questions, email ask@mountainsoftllc.com.

Common Steps

  • Open the app and select a station before adding the widget.
  • If arrivals look stale, pull down in the app to refresh.
  • On iOS 17 or later, tap the widget refresh icon to refresh Home Screen widget arrivals.
  • If a Lock Screen widget does not appear, confirm you are using the latest installed build.

Transit Data

MetroLens uses publicly available WMATA rail data through a MetroLens API proxy. Arrival predictions can change quickly and may be unavailable during WMATA outages or maintenance.
